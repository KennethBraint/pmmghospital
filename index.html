    Cooperatives are by nature bastions of relief. According to the international Cooperative Authority, " Cooperatives ar based on the values of self responsibility, democracy, equity, andsolidarity. In the tradition of their founders, cooperative members believe in the ethical values of honesty, openness, social responisbility and caring for others" (2016). Cooperative were started to help its members achieve in life what they could not do individually and separately.

    Hospitals are also institutions of care. People go to hospitals to find comfort, aid and respite. Hospitals are mandated to be hospitable; treatment should not be base on gender, sexual preference, race nationality, politacal affiliation or religious beliefs.

    Therefore, being a cooperative hospital entails and demands much more from us compared to the two usually seperate entities. The Challenge has been given Together, we will move forward, upward and onward as we live by our calling to be Campions for Those in Need!

    Congratulations to everyone for the self-less cooperation and utmost labor of love. "We Care for Life"
